Twilight Verse

In twilight's gentle, fading hue
Whispers of the evening dew.
Stars adorn the velvet sky,
A cosmic dance, as time drifts by.
Moonlight weaves a silver thread,
Through dreams where fantasies are bred.
Nature's symphony, a soft refrain,
Echoes of a world untamed.
Blossoms bow in the pale moon's grace,
A tranquil night, a sacred space.
Silent symphony, a lullaby,
As the universe hums, oh so high.
Embrace the night, let worries wane,
In the celestial dance, find peace again.
A poem spun from twilight's art, A masterpiece written in the heart.
